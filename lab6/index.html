Lab 6 - Arrays and Objects

Challenge

The challenge of this lab was to expand on the car data we had from Lab 5 and store that in an object. We also had to store all our common forms of transportation into an array so that they could be displayed on our lab page.

Problems

I found that there was strange spacing within the array, not having a space after each element, so I added a space to the end of each individual element to make it more aesthetic. I also got confused when trying to call the myMainRide object in the Google Chrome console, because I didn't realize that the object couldn't understand the concatenation I was trying to do.

Reflection

Overall, this assignment went well for me. I was able to really solidify the knowledge of different properties of arrays and objects through experimentation in the console and VSCode.
